## How to add a real review

1. Copy this file into `src/content/tech-reviews/` and rename it, e.g. `moondrop-blessing3-review.md`.
2. Fill in the front-matter. `title` is the review title. `date` is the publish date (`YYYY-MM-DD`). `product` is the product name. `summary` is one or two sentences for the list card. `draft` should be `false` (or removed) once it's ready to publish.
3. Write the review body below: an honest take.
